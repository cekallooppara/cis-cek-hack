ORAL POLIO VACCINE(OPV)

When to take: 4 doses are given each of two drops.
1. Birth dose(OPV0)
2. at 6 weeks(OPV1)
3. at 10 weeks(OPV2)
4. at 14 weeks(OPV3)
Not taking OPV at right time can lead to Poliomyelitis-a crippling disease of the brain and spinal cord.
Protect your child from this deadly disease by taking vaccine at the right time.
